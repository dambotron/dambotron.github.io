The Lore Behind the Slice

The Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles' love for pizza wasn't actually prominent in the original, dark, gritty Mirage comic books created by Kevin Eastman and Peter Laird. In the comics, they ate it occasionally, but it wasn't an obsession.

The 1987 Cartoon Changed Everything

When the 1987 animated series launched, the creators needed to make the turtles more relatable and kid-friendly. What do kids love? Pizza! The cartoon heavily leaned into the joke of the turtles ordering pizzas to the sewer and requesting absolutely disgusting topping combinations.

The Live-Action "Pizza Dude"

In the 1990 live-action movie, pizza was a central plot device. The famous "Pizza Dude's got 30 seconds" scene cemented Domino's (and later Pizza Hut in other promotions) into TMNT history. The delivery driver having to drop the pizza down a sewer grate is an iconic moment in cinema history.
