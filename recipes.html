Totally Tubular TMNT Pizza Recipes

Ready to eat like a true Ninja Turtle? Forget plain cheese. The best Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle pizzas require a strong stomach and a love for the weird. Here are some classic combinations pulled straight from the shows.

1. The Michelangelo Classic: Marshmallow and Pepperoni

Perhaps the most famous TMNT pizza of all time. The salty, savory pepperoni perfectly balances the gooey, sweet melted marshmallow.

  • Base: Standard NY-style dough with classic tomato sauce.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella.
  • Toppings: Pepperoni slices and mini marshmallows (add marshmallows halfway through baking so they don't burn).

2. The Donatello: Peanut Butter and Clam

Donnie is the brains of the operation, but this pizza choice is madness. The thick texture of peanut butter mixed with salty ocean clams.

  • Base: Thick crust. Skip the tomato sauce.
  • Sauce: Creamy peanut butter (melted slightly for easy spreading).
  • Toppings: Canned chopped clams (drained) and a sprinkle of parmesan.
